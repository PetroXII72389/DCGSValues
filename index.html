DCGS

DCGS or "Dr Challoner's Grammar School" is an excellent school in Amersham in South-East England. Here we motivate students to take an interest in their own education and emphasize key values. Our main values are made clear in our motte "Excellence with Integrity" as the two values that the school promotes above all others are excellence as integrity. This is because without excellence their is no success; without integrity excellence is worthless. As well as this we also promote interactional skills such as empathy and kindness such that our students develop into empathetic and kind young men. We guarentee that all students leaving Challoners pride themselves on their kindness and academic excellence.

We also stand out for exceptional results in GCSEs and A-Levels that are some of the greatest in the entire country. This is as a result of excellent staff who truly care about their students and students that value their own education.

Key Values

Key Value Explanation How We Promote This
Commitment Challoners students are committed to their work and other parts of their school and personal life. This schools promotes commitment to learning by teaching students to motivate themselves.
Teamwork Challoners students can work effectively as a team whoever they are put with. We promote teamwork by helping students to work together whenever possible.
Integrity Students at DCGS value integrity above all else as is summarised in our motto: "Excellence with Integrity" We teach students to value integrity above all else in their work and lives.
Humility Our students are humble and never boast to others of their own achievements. Our school teaches humility by teaching students that they do not have to show off.
Excellence Our students excel academically with some of the best GCSE and A-Level results in the country. We promote excellence throught an excellent teaching staff who make sure students are making good progress in their academic studies.
Perseverance Challoners students always persevere regardless of what obstacles come their way in life. DCGS promotes perseverance by teaching students to keep trying no matter what happens.
